About Us

Founded in 1964 by Masaru Ibuka, Sony strives do what has never been done before. Our founder has said that the purpose of setting up the company was to "establish an ideal factory that stresses a spirit of freedom and open mindedness that will, through technology, contribute to Japanese culture." And these are values we keep in thought as we design and value dearly.
